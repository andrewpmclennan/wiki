====== Red builder notes ======
Created Tuesday 16 December 2025

Creating a repo based on a pre-existing workspace
NB Both of these need to be full paths

'''
r workspace <NAME OF THE NEW WORKSPACE> --clone <NAME OF THE OLD WORKSPACE>

'''
NB this is for cloning workspaces, not the images

Creating a new workspace

r new <PLATFORM> <YOUR_NEW_NAME_FOR_CONTAINER> <WORKSPACE_PATH>

Nightly images

These are located in 
[[/build/jenkins/nightly]]

When you login to a new container, your environment may not be properly setup.

Run

'''r-setup_environment.sh''' 

Test with croot

then

'''r-relunch.sh'''
